# PRUVIQ Business Model (Draft)

> Last updated: 2026-02-15
> Model: 100% Free service, revenue from exchange referrals

## 1. Revenue Model

### Primary: Exchange Referral Commissions

| Exchange | Commission Rate | Duration | Link Type |
|----------|----------------|----------|-----------|
| Binance | 20-41% of fees | 12 months | Futures referral |
| Bybit | 30-50% of fees | Lifetime | Partner link |
| OKX | Up to 50% | Varies | Affiliate |

### How Referrals Integrate (Naturally, Not Pushy)

```
User Journey:
1. Discovery → Find PRUVIQ (free simulation platform)
2. Value → Simulate strategies, read education content
3. Conviction → "This strategy looks profitable, I want to trade it"
4. Natural moment → "Which exchange? PRUVIQ compares fees + offers discounts"
5. Signup → User gets fee discount, PRUVIQ earns commission
```

**Touchpoints:**
- `/fees` page: Exchange comparison with transparent referral links
- Blog tutorials: API setup guides naturally mention exchange signup
- Strategy results: "Want to trade this? Compare exchanges"
- Telegram pinned: "Keeps PRUVIQ free" disclosure

**Rules:**
- Value first, referral second (never before user gets value)
- Transparent disclosure on every referral link
- User retains full choice (alternatives always shown)
- No pressure language

## 2. Free Tier (100% of Features)

ALL users get everything. No paywalls.

- Strategy simulation (unlimited)
- 500+ coins, 2+ years data
- Realistic cost modeling (fees + slippage)
- Educational content (blog)
- Strategy library (including failed strategies)
- Market context data
- Community access (Telegram)

## 3. Revenue Projections (Conservative)

### Per-User Economics

Average crypto futures trader:
- Monthly volume: $50K (US) / $200K (Korea)
- Fees: ~0.05% = $25-$100/month
- PRUVIQ commission (25%): $6.25-$25/month per active trader

### Conversion Funnel

```
Free Members → 20% active → 30% signup → 50% active trader
1,000 → 200 → 60 → 30 active traders
30 x $6.25 = $187.50/month
```

### Revenue Targets

| Members | Active Traders | Monthly | Annual | Timeline |
|---------|---------------|---------|--------|----------|
| 500 | 15 | $94 | $1,128 | Month 6 |
| 1,000 | 30 | $188 | $2,256 | Month 9 |
| 5,000 | 150 | $938 | $11,256 | Month 18 |
| 10,000 | 300 | $1,875 | $22,500 | Month 24 |

**Break-even**: ~25 active traders ($150/month) = Month 6-9
**Costs**: $1-6/month (self-hosted infrastructure)

### Korean Market Accelerator

Korean traders average 4x volume ($200K/month).
- 1,000 Korean traders = $25,000/month (vs $6,250 US)
- Strategy: Launch English → Korean expansion as primary growth lever
