## Description (250 words max)

PRUVIQ is a free crypto strategy backtesting platform built for traders who want data, not promises.

**Build strategies visually** — Combine Bollinger Bands, RSI, MACD, EMA, Stochastic, ADX, ATR, and more with AND/OR logic. No coding required.

**Test on real data** — Run your strategy across 535+ Binance USDT perpetual futures with 2+ years of 1-hour candle data. Results include realistic fees, slippage, and position sizing.

**See what actually works** — We publish 5 pre-built strategies on the site, including 2 that lost money. Our verified strategy (BB Squeeze SHORT) runs on a real Binance Futures account with $3,000 capital. Every trade is public. Every loss is visible.

**Compare strategies side-by-side** — Same conditions, same data, different approaches. See win rates, profit factors, drawdowns, and equity curves for all 5 strategies simultaneously.

**Learn from real data** — 17 educational articles covering backtesting methodology, technical indicators, risk management, and fee optimization. All bilingual (English + Korean).

Built by a solo developer who puts real money where the data is.
