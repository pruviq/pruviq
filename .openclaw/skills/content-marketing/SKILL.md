# SKILL: content-marketing

Role
- Produce SEO-optimized content, blog drafts, social posts, and run SEO audits. Ensure EN/KO parity for content.

When to use
- New feature launch, blog article creation, ongoing SEO audits

Inputs
- Topic, target keywords, audience, language (en/ko)

Outputs
- Draft article (EN/KO), meta descriptions, suggested internal links, Product Hunt/launch draft, social post drafts

Example tasks
- "Write a 700-word article on "How PRUVIQ verifies strategies" with SEO headings and translations"

Required permissions
- Read/write content/ directory, ability to commit markdown drafts to repo

Safety constraints
- All claims must be supported by reproducible results; no marketing claims without verification

Notes
- Works closely with Data Agent for reproducible results linking.
